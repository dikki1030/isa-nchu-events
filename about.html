Who We Are

The Indian Students Association (ISA) at National Chung Hsing University is a home away from home. We are a student-run volunteer group dedicated to helping you survive and thrive in Taiwan.

New Student Guidance

Confused about ARCs, SIM cards, or dorms? We help new students answer questions about life in Taichung, navigating the NCHU campus, and settling into Taiwan smoothly.
